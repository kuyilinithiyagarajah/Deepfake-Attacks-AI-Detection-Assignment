## 📋 About This Assignment

This individual assignment explores the rapidly evolving world of **deepfake technology** — from how it is created using AI to how it can be detected and prevented. As synthetic media becomes increasingly realistic and accessible, understanding these threats is critical for modern cybersecurity professionals.

---

## 🧠 Topics Covered

### 🔍 What Are Deepfakes?
- Definition and origins of deepfake technology
- How **GANs (Generative Adversarial Networks)** and **Diffusion Models** work
- Why deepfakes are a serious cybersecurity threat

### 📈 Evolution of Deepfake Technology
| Phase | Period | Key Development |
|-------|--------|----------------|
| 🟡 Phase 1 | 2017–2018 | Consumer deepfakes via DeepFaceLab & FaceSwap |
| 🟠 Phase 2 | 2019–2020 | Audio-visual fusion & First-Order Motion Model |
| 🔴 Phase 3 | 2021–2022 | Real-time deepfakes & Stable Diffusion release |
| 🟣 Phase 4 | 2023–Now | Foundation model era & industrial-scale synthesis |

### 🛡️ AI-Based Detection Methods
- 🖼️ **CNN Spatial Analysis** — detects pixel-level visual artefacts (~95% accuracy)
- ⏱️ **LSTM Temporal Analysis** — checks blinking, lip sync & motion over time (~90% accuracy)
- 🔭 **Vision Transformer (ViT)** — global attention across full facial images (~97% accuracy)
- 📊 **Frequency Domain Analysis** — detects hidden AI frequency fingerprints (~88% accuracy)
- 💓 **Biometric rPPG Analysis** — detects heartbeat pulse signals in real faces (~85% accuracy)
- 🔗 **Multimodal Fusion** — combines all methods for highest accuracy (~98% accuracy)

### 🚀 Future Developments
- ⛓️ **Blockchain Content Provenance** (C2PA standard)
- 🔒 **Federated Learning** for privacy-preserving detector training
- 📱 **Real-Time On-Device Detection** via smartphone NPUs
- ⚔️ **Adversarially Hardened Detection Systems**
- 🌊 **Universal AI Content Watermarking** (SynthID by Google DeepMind)
- ⚖️ **Legal & Regulatory Frameworks** (EU AI Act 2024, DEEPFAKES Accountability Act)
